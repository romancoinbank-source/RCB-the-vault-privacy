Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Roman Coin Bank Ltd ("RCB", "we", "us", or "our") operates the RCB: The Vault mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and what rights you have under applicable data protection law, including the UK GDPR and EU GDPR.

1. Who We Are

RCB: The Vault is a numismatic marketplace and collection management platform for collectors of ancient and historical coins. We are the data controller responsible for your personal data.

Contact: legal@rcb-coins.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the platform:

  • Account information: username, email address, password (hashed), country of residence
  • Profile information: avatar or profile photo, biography, public/private profile setting
  • Collection data: coin details (name, year, country, condition, estimated value, purchase price, photos, notes) that you choose to enter
  • Marketplace data: listing details, asking price, PayPal email address (for receiving payments), postage cost, sale type
  • Transaction data: bids placed, offers made, completed sales, seller performance records
  • Shipping & billing addresses: collected when you activate a seller account or make a purchase, used solely for transaction fulfilment
  • Communications: messages sent between buyers and sellers through the in-app messaging system
  • Device data: push notification token (to send you relevant alerts), device type and OS version for technical support
  • Usage data: app interactions used to improve performance and detect fraud (no advertising profiling)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Enabling marketplace transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Sending push notifications relevant to your activity (bids, messages, listing approvals)
  • Moderating listings and enforcing community standards
  • Processing seller registration fees and listing fees via PayPal
  • Resolving disputes between users
  • Complying with legal obligations (fraud prevention, GDPR requests)
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with data brokers.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for (account management, marketplace transactions)
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention, platform security, improving the service
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws
  • Consent: Push notifications (you can withdraw consent at any time in device settings)

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Other users: Your public profile, listings, and reviews are visible to other RCB users as per your privacy settings. Your full shipping address is only shared with a buyer/seller after a completed transaction.
  • PayPal: When you make or receive a payment, you interact directly with PayPal's platform. We do not store your PayPal credentials — only the email address you provide for receiving payments.
  • Push notification providers: Expo (Expo Push Notification Service) is used to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or fraud-prevention purposes (e.g., completed transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for financial compliance).

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Passwords stored as one-way hashes (never in plain text)
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure server infrastructure with access controls
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) available for all accounts
  • Session management with remote sign-out capability

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect unauthorised access to your account, please contact us immediately at legal@rcb-coins.com.

8. Children's Privacy

RCB: The Vault is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

9. International Transfers

Our servers are located within the UK/EU. If data is transferred outside these regions (e.g., via Expo's push notification infrastructure), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with UK GDPR and EU GDPR requirements.

10. Cookies & Tracking

The RCB mobile app does not use cookies. We use minimal, anonymised analytics to understand app performance. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics platforms that profile you for advertising purposes.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

🔒 Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent for push notifications at any time in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.
